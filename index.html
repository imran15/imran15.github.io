Welcome


Short bio: Muhammad Imran currently works as a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Social Computing group at the Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI). Prior to QCRI, Imran was a PhD candidate at the University of Trento, where he received his PhD degree in Computer Science in 2013. During his PhD, he also taught courses related to the Web Service Design and Web Engineering areas in the University of Trento from 2009-2012.

Imran's current research interests include Social Computing, Crisis Computing, Crowdsourcing, and Web Engineering research areas. Combination of his research interests can be seen live at AIDR (Artificial Intelligence for Disaster Response) project.

Imran received a Master of Science in Computer Science degree with a distinction from Mohammad Ali Jinnah University, Islamabad, Pakistan in 2007. He is also an Oracle Certified Professional (OCP). He obtained his OCP certification in 2004 from the Oracle Corporation, California, USA.
